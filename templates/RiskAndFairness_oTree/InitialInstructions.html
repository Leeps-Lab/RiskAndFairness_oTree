General Instructions

Please pay careful attention to the instructions. You will be asked to make decisions in a series of different tasks and precise rules will be provided before you start each type of task.

Your final earnings today will depend partly on your decisions, partly on the decisions of others and partly on chance. At the end of the experiment, you will be paid in cash. All payments will be made in private using and envelope. Also, you will receive additional {{ participation_fee }} as a participation fee.

During the experiment we will use Experimental Tokens instead of dollars. At the end of the experiment, your earnings in tokens will be translated into dollars. You will receive 1 dollar for every 5 tokens you have earned in the session. It is important that you do not talk or in any way try to communicate with other people during the session. If you have a question, raise your hand and an experimenter will attend to your station to answer your question privately. The experiment should be finished in approximately one hour.

Pairs

At the beginning of the experiment, you will be randomly and anonymously matched with one other participant to form a pair. Within each pair there will be one person with the role of Decider and another person with the role of Partner. These roles will be assigned randomly within each pair, and will remain for the entire session. Only choices made by the Decider will determine final payoffs. Partner choices will not determine anyoneâ€™s payoffs. Roles, however, will be disclosed after the last decision has been made. Finally, participants will not learn the identity of his/her counterpart at any moment of the session.

Tasks, Decision Rounds and Earnings

This experiment will consist of a series of decision rounds of {{ Constants.number_types_of_tasks }} different types of tasks. Each kind of task will be explained later. At the end of all decisions, your role (Decider or Partner) will be disclosed. Then the computer will randomly select one choice made by the Decider and generate payoffs for both member of the pair based on that selected choice. The selected choice as well as the final payoffs it generates will be disclosed to both participants.
